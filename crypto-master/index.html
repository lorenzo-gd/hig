Who is HIG

Born in an enchanted grove, HIG was always different from other leprechauns. While they sought pots of gold at the end of rainbows, HIG learned that true wealth was in the "hold" - the act of keeping, cherishing, and seeing as it grows in value.

Motivation

HIG's quest is to spread the wisdom of "Hold is Gold," helping others find what is truly valuable in their lives, while still keeping a keen eye on any real gold that might come his way. His adventures often involve resolving dilemmas where individuals have lost sight of what's important, reminding them that sometimes, the hold you have on something is worth more than the gold itself.
